# Grounding Techniques

## 5-4-3-2-1 Sensory Exercise
Name 5 things you can see, 4 you can touch, 3 you can hear, 2 you can smell, 1 you can taste. Takes about 60 seconds.

## Box Breathing
Inhale 4 counts, hold 4, exhale 4, hold 4. Repeat 4 times. Resets the nervous system.

## 4-7-8 Breathing
Inhale 4 counts, hold 7, exhale 8. Do this 3-4 times for anxiety.

## Cold Water Reset
Splash cold water on your face or hold ice cubes. Activates the dive reflex and slows heart rate.
